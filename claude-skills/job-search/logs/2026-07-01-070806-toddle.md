# Job Application Log — Toddle — 2026-07-01

## Job Description

We are looking for a passionate and motivated product engineer who partners with us to build intuitive, scalable, and delightful user experiences for teachers, students, and parents.

As a product Engineer, you will contribute to designing, developing, and deploying high-quality product. You will collaborate closely with engineers, product managers, designers, educators, and gain exposure to real-world usage in schools.

This role offers hands-on ownership, continuous technical growth and product understanding in a fast-growing SaaS environment.

What you'll achieve in your first 3 months

Understand the codebase, backend architecture, and core product workflows
Build strong fundamentals in PostgreSQL, GraphQL, and basic query writing
Start contributing to feature development, production fixes, and basic query optimization
What you'll achieve in your first 6 months

Independently deliver major backend features by writing clean, modular, and optimized code
Become comfortable with query optimization, and writing scripts for analytics and database migrations
Participate in code reviews and collaborate cross-functionally with Engineering, QA, and Product teams
What you'll achieve in your first 12 months

Own backend modules end-to-end, coordinating closely with Product and Support teams through scoping, design reviews, development, release, and post-release support
Get the opportunity to work on backend infrastructure projects or contribute to other backend services
Contribute to onboarding, technical knowledge sharing, and continuous improvement of engineering processes
Must have Skills:

Frameworks & Technologies - Node.js, Express & GraphQL, PostgreSQL
Tools & Platforms - Git, Knex, GitHub Copilot or other AI-assisted development tools
Experience with scripting and data analysis tasks
Strong problem-solving skills and eagerness to learn
Ability to write maintainable, readable, and scalable code
Good to have:

Familiarity with the AWS ecosystem (Lambda, SQS, CloudWatch, API Gateway, etc.)
Experience with background jobs, queues, and workers
Exposure to additional data stores such as Redis or ClickHouse or Elastic Search
Basic understanding of React and Apollo Client
Product mindset with empathy for end users and strong collaboration skills
Past working experience in SaaS or product based companies
Is this someone that looks like you?

Education: B.E/B.Tech degree
Experience: 1 to 3 years of relevant working experience (SaaS preferred)
Soft skills: Having a bias towards action, a good sense of design, empathy, and good communication skills
We deeply value building the right culture at Toddle, and these are a few things that we look for in each hire – Coach-ability, Curiosity, Ownership, Hustle and Humility
